      Frequently Asked Questions

      What is the passing score for the NJ driving test?

      You need to answer at least 80% of the questions correctly to pass the test.

      How do I book my NJ knowledge test?

      You can book your test online through the official MVC knowledge test page.

      What documents do I need for the knowledge test?

      You will need your 6 Points of ID, proof of address, and your social security number.

      What happens if I fail the test?

      If you fail, you must wait at least 7 days before you can retake the test.
